My Time



About Us

MyTime is a task managing webapp designed to be simple and easy to use for students to track their time. We aim to help students improve their time management skills and reflect on their progress.


Our team is made up of seven freshmen from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

The project managers are Caren Zeng and Travis Newgren, both Computer Science majors.

The front-end team includes Richa Misra, Computer Science major, Mia Zhang, intended business major, and Helena Chi, Computer Science and Linguistics major.

The back-end duo is Zhuoyue Wang, Statistics and Computer Science major, and Wanyu Bi, Environmental Science major.

Both teams worked together under the guidance and supervision of our team's two project managers.
